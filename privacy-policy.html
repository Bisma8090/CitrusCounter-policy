Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 02-06-2025

At CitrusCounter, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect and how we use it when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following user information for account functionality:

  • Name – provided during signup
  • Phone number – used for login and communication

This data is used strictly within the app and is not shared with any third parties.

2. Image Uploads

Users are asked to upload four images of citrus trees to analyze and generate a summary report. These images are sent securely to our server for processing and are not stored permanently or shared.

3. Data Security

We ensure that all collected information is transmitted securely. We do not store any data longer than necessary and do not share any personal information with others.

4. Third-Party Services

CitrusEye does not use any third-party services that collect or track user data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be displayed within the app and take effect immediately upon posting.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at admin@citrusCounter.com.
